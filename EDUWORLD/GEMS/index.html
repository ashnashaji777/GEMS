FAQ

What is the MBA Professional Program?

▼

The MBA Professional Program is a specially designed MBA program for people who want to take challenges in life by working in companies along with their studies. This gives them a real opportunity to understand the real working atmosphere before completing the MBA.

How is the Professional MBA different from Normal MBA?

▼

In a normal MBA, you spend two years in the classroom, and after that, you may be ready for placement or industries. However, in a Professional MBA, you are learning and simultaneously experiencing the work atmosphere in MNCs, corporates, Pvt Ltd companies, and organizations.

What is the duration of Professional MBA?

▼

The duration is two years. In the first three months, you will be in the classroom, and for the next 1.8 years, you will be working in companies.

What are the major benefits of Professional MBA?

▼

The student earns the following four advantages: 1. 2 Year Full Time MBA 2. 1.9 Year Full Time Experience 3. 1.9 Year Full Salary 4. You will be placed as Senior Level after completing of your MBA
