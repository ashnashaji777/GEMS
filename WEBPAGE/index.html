PROFESSIONAL MBA (WORK & STUDY)


Are you Confused about going for Job or Doing MBA after your Degree ???


We have the perfect solution for you! The MBA – Professional program is designed for individuals who want to work and study simultaneously, without having to choose one over the other. It offers Education and work combined, offering practical and theoretical learning. This helps to develop key skills needed to advance in your career while building a strong academic foundation. MBA will help you to explore new job profiles without significant risks. Instead of taking a leap of faith, you can equip yourself with the necessary skills and knowledge to take up new responsibilities. It helps to increase your chances of getting faster and consistent promotions. It is the gateway to shift the career path and enhance the networking.

Why to Choose This Program?

  • No Need to Leave Your Job: Continue working while pursuing your MBA. The program is structured to allow you to balance your professional and academic life.
  • Financial Flexibility: Don’t let financial commitments hold you back. You can earn while you learn, making it easier to manage your tuition fees and living expenses.
  • Full-Time MBA: Despite the flexibility, this program offers the rigor of a full-time MBA, designed to provide holistic development.
  • Corporate Connect: Gain valuable insights and networking opportunities that will accelerate your professional growth, preparing you for leadership roles in the corporate world.
  • Real-World Experience: Apply what you learn in the classroom to your job, enhancing both your academic and professional performance.

With the MBA – Professional (Earn While You Learn) program, you can accelerate your career without missing out on the opportunity to earn your master’s degree.
